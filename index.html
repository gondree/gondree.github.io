Recent News

CS expands the SMC Program

The SSU CS department is helping expand the Koret-supported Sonoma Mountain Connection program to include computer science. This is a unique interdisplinary program designed to promote student success in early-career STEM fields, providing mentors, support and enrichment during their first year at SSU. I'm happy to support and help develop the program!

Virtual Red Chair at SSU

We've been participating in NCWIT's Sit With Me campaign through SSU's Women in Tech group over the past year.

When we went virtual, I decided to publish a Spark AR filter for instagram stories, so you can have the red chair in your living room, or grow it the size of a house. You can scale it to reflect how much room your organization is willing to make at the table for women in technology!
